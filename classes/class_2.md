## class Vegetable
---
### attributes:

  * type (string)
  * color (string)
  * is_raw (boolean)
  * is_dirty (boolean)  
  * seasoning_used (array)
  * number_of_pieces (integer)
---
### methods:  
  * wash (changes is_dirty to false)
  * season (adds to seasoning_used)
  * cook (changes is_raw to false)
  * chop (increases pieces)
